Professional Profile

Trusted Strategic Leadership with Global Reach

Suranga brings over 17 years of multifaceted experience across Sri Lanka, Singapore, and Australia. His leadership blends strategic advisory insight, project governance discipline, and commercial acumen — making him a trusted partner for clients who demand clarity, protection, and confidence in their project decisions.

As a specialist in client-side advisory, Suranga guides owners and investors through complex design, commercial, and delivery environments — ensuring decisions are well-informed, risks are controlled early, and project objectives remain aligned at all times. His international exposure brings a deeper understanding of global standards, contract practices, and structured governance models used in mature construction markets.

With a foundation in project management excellence and a commitment to transparent, intelligent client advocacy, Suranga has built PROJEX on principles of technical rigor, commercial insight, and unwavering client protection.
