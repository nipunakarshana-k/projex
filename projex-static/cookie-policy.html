Cookies
Last updated: 02 April 2026

This policy explains what cookies are, why we use them, and the simple choices you have.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember settings and improve your browsing experience.

2. Types We Use

Essential Cookies
Needed for core site functions like navigation and security.
Performance Cookies
Help us understand how the website is used so we can improve it.
Preference Cookies
Remember your choices for a smoother experience.

3. Managing Cookies

You can change cookie settings in your browser at any time. Disabling essential cookies may affect some site features.
