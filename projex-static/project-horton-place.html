No. 77, Horton Place, Colombo 07

Client Advisory & Project Management — Samudhi Group, Sri Lanka

Project Overview

A Landmark Residential Development in Colombo 07

No. 77, Horton Place is a landmark residential development located in the heart of Colombo 07, one of Sri Lanka's most prestigious addresses. The project demanded a high level of commercial discipline, structured advisory, and coordinated project management from the very outset.

Project Excellence Pvt Ltd was engaged by Samudhi Group to provide independent client-side advisory and project management services. Our role was to ensure the project aligned with the client's commercial objectives — protecting their interests across cost, time, quality, and risk at every stage of the development.

We provided strategic client advisory, coordinated design consultants across all disciplines, reviewed technical and commercial risks, and supported critical decision-making to ensure the project moved forward with clarity and structured governance.
