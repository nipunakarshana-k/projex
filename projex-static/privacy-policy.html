Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information when you engage with Project Excellence.

Data & Privacy
Last updated: 02 April 2026

Project Excellence Pvt Ltd is committed to handling personal information responsibly and transparently. This page outlines what we collect, why we collect it, and the choices available to you.

On This Page

1. Introduction 2. Information We Collect 3. How We Use Information 4. Information Sharing 5. Data Security 6. Your Rights 7. Cookies 8. Third-Party Links 9. Policy Updates 10. Contact Us

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Project Excellence Pvt Ltd ("we", "us", "our") collects, stores, uses, and protects personal information provided through our website and professional services.

2. Information We Collect

Depending on your interaction with us, we may collect:

  • Identity and contact details such as name, email address, and phone number.
  • Business information such as company name, role, and project context.
  • Technical data such as browser type, device type, pages visited, and general location indicators.
  • Information submitted through enquiry forms, calls, and project correspondence.

3. How We Use Information

  • To respond to enquiries and deliver requested advisory services.
  • To manage engagements, proposals, and project communications.
  • To improve website content, usability, and service quality.
  • To meet legal, regulatory, and contractual obligations.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Information may only be shared when required to:

  • Support operational services through trusted partners under confidentiality obligations.
  • Comply with legal processes or enforce legitimate rights.
  • Protect individuals, clients, or our business from unlawful or harmful activity.

5. Data Security

We apply reasonable technical and organisational measures to reduce risks of unauthorised access, misuse, alteration, or disclosure.

No internet-based method of transmission or storage is completely secure. While we take strong precautions, absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

6. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request to:

  • Access information we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Request deletion where retention is no longer required.
  • Withdraw consent for non-essential communications.

7. Cookies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to support core site functionality, user experience, and analytics. You can manage cookie settings via your browser.

8. Third-Party Links

Our site may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for content, privacy practices, or policies of external services.

9. Policy Updates

We may revise this policy from time to time. Any updates will be published on this page with the revised date shown above.
