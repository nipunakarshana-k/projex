Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information when you engage with Project Excellence.

Data & Privacy
Last updated: 02 April 2026

Project Excellence Pvt Ltd is committed to handling personal information responsibly and transparently. This page outlines what we collect, why we collect it, and the choices available to you.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on your interaction with us, we may collect:

  • Identity and contact details such as name, email address, and phone number.
  • Business information such as company name, role, and project context.
  • Technical data such as browser type, device type, pages visited, and general location indicators.
  • Information submitted through enquiry forms, calls, and project correspondence.

2. How We Use Information

  • To respond to enquiries and deliver requested advisory services.
  • To manage engagements, proposals, and project communications.
  • To improve website content, usability, and service quality.
  • To meet legal, regulatory, and contractual obligations.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Information may be shared only when required for lawful service delivery or legal compliance.

4. Data Security

We apply reasonable technical and organisational measures to reduce risks of unauthorised access, misuse, alteration, or disclosure.

5. Cookies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to support core site functionality, user experience, and analytics. You can manage cookie settings via your browser.

6. Contact

For questions about privacy matters, contact us at Info@projex.lk.
